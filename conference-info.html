Who Should Attend

Who Should Attend?

This conference will be of interest to any clinician or health-related professionals and academics in the medical and allied health science, researchers, students, other professionals and stakeholders. Our annual conference strives to offer great networking opportunities and the possibility for participants to meet and interact with leading academics, researchers, practitioners, colleagues, and friends as well as sponsors and exhibitors. This hybrid conference is expected to draw participants from Africa and other continents from diverse backgrounds and locations.

Book Now

AfIHC is an international peer reviewed conference for health-related researchers, practitioners, academics and students at all levels.

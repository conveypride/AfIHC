Aim, Vision, Mission

Africa Interdisciplinary Health Conference (AfIHC)

Our Aim

  1. • To showcase research and best practices in a wide range of health-related disciplines across Africa and beyond.
  2. • To provide a forum for networking and collaboration among health-related practitioners, academics, researchers, and students.
  3. • To engage with and learn from health leaders around the World.

Our Vision

Our vision is to promote a strong collaboration among health-related stakeholders for evidence-based healthcare delivery in Africa.

Our Mission

AfIHC's mission is to advance the understanding of health through interdisciplinary research to promote evidence-based practice among healthcare providers across Africa. It seeks to provide the opportunity for Health related researchers, practitioners, and students to network and establish mutually beneficial south-south and south-north collaborations. AfiHC is committed to translating research into practice in Africa.
